Meet Anthias

Formerly known as Screenly OSE, Anthias is the most popular digital signage solution for the Raspberry Pi.

What is Anthias

Anthias free digital signage software that is backed by our community and maintained by Screenly, Inc. Anthias runs on dedicated hardware - the Raspberry Pi. We launched Anthias back in 2012 on GitHub, and it now powers thousands of screens around the world.

While we do not offer support for Anthias, there are several self-support resources where you can ask for help and troubleshoot. You can open an issue on Screenly OSE's GitHub page, post on the Forum, or post on Screenly OSE's thread on the Raspberry Pi Foundation's forum.
